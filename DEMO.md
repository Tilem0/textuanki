## Color Scheme

TextuAnki uses a clean, readable color scheme:

- **Primary**: Blue - Headers, borders, emphasis
- **Accent**: Cyan - Important elements, stats
- **Success**: Green - Positive actions, "Good" rating
- **Warning**: Yellow - "Hard" rating
- **Error**: Red - Delete actions, "Again" rating
- **Text**: White/Gray - Main content
- **Background**: Dark - Easy on the eyes

## Design Philosophy

1. **Clean & Minimal**: No clutter, focus on content
2. **Keyboard-First**: Fast navigation without mouse
3. **Consistent**: Same patterns throughout
4. **Informative**: Always show available actions
5. **Responsive**: Quick feedback on all actions

---

*These are ASCII representations. The actual app uses rich TUI components with colors and borders!*
